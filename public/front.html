Art Gallery Database

Welcome

Our database serves as a platform for art galleries to oversee logistical information about galleries, exhibits, artwork, artists, and customers. Users can add, update, and delete galleries, exhibits, artwork, etc. from the database. Users can also filter the results that appear as well as decide which attributes are displayed. In addition, the application provides the ability to determine useful statistics that give galleries insight into their database. For instance, one can view which artworks are featured in certain exhibits, the number of artworks in each gallery, customers who have visited every exhibit in a gallery, and more.
